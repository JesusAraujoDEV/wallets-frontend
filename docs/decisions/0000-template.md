## Context

What forced this decision. The constraint, the problem, the trigger. Two or three sentences. No history of the project — just the situation that made this decision necessary.

## Decision

One sentence stating what was decided. Imperative voice.

Then up to a paragraph of detail: which patterns, which contracts, which dependencies. Be specific enough that a reader six months from now can recognize the decision in the code.

## Considered alternatives

At least one rejected option. For each: one line of what it was, one line of why it lost.

## Consequences

- **Positive**: what this enables.
- **Negative**: what this costs (build time, complexity, vendor lock-in, learning curve).
- **Neutral**: what changes but is neither win nor loss.

## Migration notes

Only if the decision changes existing code. List the steps and the affected modules. Otherwise omit this section.
